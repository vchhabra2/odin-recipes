Butter Chicken

Description

Origin

Butter chicken is an iconic Indian dish. It is the bread and butter (pun absolutely intended) of the Indian and Indian diaspora communties. Also, it is a highly revered and praised dish in the Pakistani community as well, given the close-knit ties between North Indian and Pakistani cuisines.

Butter chicken is a hybrid and continuation of the long-standing tradition of chicken curry and the restaurant-ization of chicken curries in North Indian cuisine.

Flavor Profile

Butter chicken has a creamy, yet spicy flavorful profile. The focus is on the Indian flavors that define chicken curries in North Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 lb chicken thighs and 0.5 lb chicken leg (bone-in)
  • 3 bunches coriander
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 spoons turmeric
  • 2 spoons garam masala
  • handful fennel seeds

Steps

Take care to follow more on feel rather than a focus simply on following the steps exactly.
  1. Grind garlic to powder form
  2. Saute garlic and turmeric on low heat for 5 min
  3. Put in chicken thighs and drumsticks to cook fully
  4. Add coriander and fennel seeds with half a stick of butter
  5. Saute until completed
